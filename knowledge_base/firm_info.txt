COMMON CLIENT QUESTIONS
=======================

Q: How long will my case take?
A: It depends on the type and complexity of your case. Simple uncontested
divorces can be finalized in 60 to 90 days. Personal injury cases typically
settle in 6 to 18 months. Criminal cases vary widely based on charge severity
and court schedules. We will give you a realistic timeline during your free consultation.

Q: What should I bring to my consultation?
A: Bring any documents related to your case. For personal injury: accident
reports, medical records, insurance information, and photos. For family law:
marriage certificate, financial records, and any existing court orders.
For criminal: any paperwork you received from law enforcement or the court.

Q: Do you handle cases outside of Houston?
A: Yes. We handle cases throughout the state of Texas. For immigration matters,
we assist clients nationwide.

Q: What if I cannot afford an attorney?
A: For personal injury cases, you pay nothing unless we win. For other case
types, we offer flexible payment plans and will discuss all fee options during
your free consultation. We never want cost to be a barrier to justice.

Q: Is my conversation confidential?
A: All information you share is kept strictly confidential and used only to
connect you with the right attorney at our firm. This chat is for intake
purposes only and does not constitute legal advice or create an attorney-client
relationship.

Q: What if the other party has already hired a lawyer?
A: That is very common and it is even more reason to have experienced
representation on your side. Contact us immediately so we can review
your situation and advise you on next steps.

Q: Can I get a second opinion on my case?
A: Absolutely. We welcome second opinions. Many clients come to us after
being unsatisfied with another firm. Your free consultation is a no-pressure
conversation with no obligation.
